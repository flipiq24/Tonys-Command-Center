# HOW TO USE THIS DOCUMENT

Every feature has three parts:
1. **WHAT TO BUILD** — what the feature does
2. **HOW TO TEST** — exact steps to verify it works
3. **ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA** — the specific things that must be true for it to pass

Do NOT mark a feature done until every acceptance criterion passes.
Run through this entire checklist at the END of the build as a final review.
