Section 01

What Is This App?

Tony's Command Center is a single point of entry for the FlipIQ leadership team to manage their entire daily operation from one dashboard.

The Problem

Tony (CEO), Ethan (COO), and Ramy (CSM) were switching between 8+ different tools daily: Gmail, Google Calendar, Slack, Linear, Google Sheets, Google Docs, phone dialer, and CRM. Context was lost. Follow-ups were missed. Tony's ADHD made this scattered workflow especially damaging to productivity.

The Solution

One app that pulls all data into a single dashboard, interconnected via Supabase/PostgreSQL, with a smart AI overlay (Claude) trained on FlipIQ's business logic. The AI can read and write to ALL connected services, giving the team a fully functioning AI assistant built into their daily workflow.

Core Capabilities

Unified Dashboard

Gmail + Calendar + Slack + Linear + CRM in one view

🤖

North Star Metric: Every Acquisition Associate closes 2 deals/month at $2,500/acquisition.
Revenue targets: $50K break-even → $100K Phase 1 → $250K Scale.

Claude Models Used:
• claude-haiku-4-5 — Fast, cheap. Used for quick classifications, drafts, scoring.
